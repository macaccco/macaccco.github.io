Abbey Road

Rock, Pop, Classic

Favourite songs: The End, Oh! Darling, Here comes the sun

9.4/10

I find that my generation dismisses this album without a proper appreciation for it. Unfortunately, this IS a great album. This album is split into two halves, the first, unorganised section and the medley. I think that the medley is better than the first section. The medley starts slow at "Because", and progressively gets more rock 'n' roll (and better), until the end.

Animals

Prog rock, Political, Epic guitar

Favourite songs: Dogs, Pigs, Sheep

10/10

Three songs. Three of the best guitar solos ever.

Animals is based on George Orwell's Animal Farm, and the three songs represent three of the main characters in Animal Farm. The Pigs represent dictators who utilise dogs to rise to power over the sheep. Each song is long (over 10 minutes) and each culminate in an epic guitar solo near the end of the song. Sheep is one of my all-time favourite songs.
